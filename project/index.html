Cyber Safety Starts With Awareness

About CyberSafe

What this website is CyberSafe is an educational single-page site focused on cyber security awareness for everyday users and small teams. It provides clear guidance, practical features, and quick checks to reduce risk.

Who it's for Students, employees, small business owners, and anyone who uses the internet and wants to improve their digital safety.

Why it matters Most breaches begin with human error — awareness and simple habits dramatically reduce exposure to phishing, credential theft, and privacy leaks.

Quick tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Verify links before clicking
